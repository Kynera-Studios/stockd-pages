# Stockd Support

Stockd is built by **Kynera Studios LLC**. We're a small team — we'll get back to you, but please give us a few days.

## Contact

**Email:** [info@kynerastudios.com](mailto:info@kynerastudios.com)

When you write in, please include:

- Your **User ID** (find it in-app at **Settings → User ID** — long-press to copy)
- A short description of what happened and what you expected
- Screenshots if you have them

We don't ask for your password (Stockd uses Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google — we never see it) and we don't ask for your subscription receipt.

## Common questions

### "I deleted my account but I'm still getting charged"

Deleting your Stockd account does not cancel your Apple subscription. The subscription is managed by Apple, not Stockd, and only you can cancel it.

To cancel:

1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top, then **Subscriptions**
3. Tap **Stockd**, then **Cancel Subscription**

Or visit [https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions](https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions).

### "I deleted my account, re-signed in, but Pro features aren't back"

Known limitation in our first release. If you had an active Stockd Pro subscription when you deleted your account, the subscription stays attached to your Apple ID but doesn't automatically re-link to your new Stockd account.

**Fix:** email us at [info@kynerastudios.com](mailto:info@kynerastudios.com) with your **new** User ID and the email address on your Apple ID. We'll re-link your subscription manually. (We'll automate this in a future update.)

### "I can't sign in"

Stockd uses Sign in with Apple and Sign in with Google. If either is failing:

- Check that you have a working internet connection
- Try force-quitting the app and reopening
- If you previously revoked Stockd's access in **Settings → Apple ID → Password & Security → Apps Using Apple ID**, you'll need to allow it again

If sign-in still fails, email us with the error message you see.

### "Setting up your kitchen…" hangs

On rare occasions, account provisioning can fail. The "Couldn't finish setting up" screen has a **Try again** button — tap it once or twice. If it still fails, take a screenshot showing the User ID and error code at the bottom of the screen and email it to us.

### "How do I share my pantry with my partner / roommates?"

Stockd Pro lets you invite up to 5 household members. Each member needs their own Stockd Pro subscription to access Pro features on their device — the subscription covers one Apple ID at a time (we don't currently support Apple Family Sharing).

To invite: **Settings → Household → Invite member** (in-app).

### "What does Stockd Pro include?"

- Unlimited AI recipe suggestions (free tier is capped at 15 / month)
- AI-generated recipe images
- Step-by-step cooking instructions
- Bulk "add all missing ingredients" from a recipe to your grocery list
- Multi-member household sharing (3+ members)

Free includes: full inventory tracking, expiration dates, barcode scanning, bulk scan, basic grocery list, 15 AI recipes / month, basic "add this recipe to my grocery list" path, and one household invite.
